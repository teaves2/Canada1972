Education

Through the “28, 800 Second” initiatives, Team Canada 1972 is educating children and adults using the stories and lessons learned from the Summit Series.

Education Curriculum

The team is working with local school boards and Canada’s Sports Hall of Fame to develop an educational curriculum for grades 2 to 12. This includes lesson plans and/or guidelines for all age levels and a complimentary, interactive online resource. Connect your brand with thousands of children through this curriculum.

Hockey Program

A grassroots hockey training curriculum for players, coaches and parents. This is a unique program that will provide coaches with the knowledge and tools to maximize the hockey experience. Includes season long training, mentorship, online interactive tools and live events. Put your brand at the forefront of this program.
